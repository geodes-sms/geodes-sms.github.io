GEODES at ESEC/FSE 2023

Published on: Dec 15, 2023

Two of our members, Martin WEYSSOW and Mouna DHAOUADI, presented their work at FSE 2023.
Mouna won third place in the Student Research Competition track.

Martin presented his work On the Usage of Continual Learning for Out-of-Distribution Generalization in Pre-trained Language Models of Code, co-authored with Xin ZHOU, Kisub KIM, David LO, and Houari SAHRAOUI. A preprint version is available here.

Mouna won third place in the Student Research Competition track for her work A Data set of Extracted Rationale from Linux Kernel Commit Messages.
